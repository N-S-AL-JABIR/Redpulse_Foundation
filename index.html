About Redpulse

We Are the Bridge Between Life and Hope

Founded in 2018 in Dhaka, Redpulse Foundation is a non-profit humanitarian organization dedicated to building a sustainable voluntary blood donation ecosystem across Bangladesh. We connect willing donors with patients facing medical emergencies — every day, around the clock.

Through awareness drives, organized donation camps, and a digital network of verified donors, we've built Bangladesh's most trusted platform for blood donation — free, fast, and life-saving.

  • 100% Voluntary & Free
    All donations are voluntary. Patients never pay for blood — ever.
  • 24/7 Emergency Network
    Our rapid response team operates round the clock for emergencies.
  • Medically Verified Donors
    Every donor is health-screened and registered with government approval.
  • Citywide Coverage
    64 partner hospitals and clinics across Dhaka and nearby districts.
